Each of your daily makeup products audited for comedogenic ingredients, fragrance, and potential irritants for acne-prone skin.
Merit Minimalist Concealer Stick
Acne-Safe
Explicitly formulated without comedogenic ingredients. Oil-free, fragrance-free, non-comedogenic, dermatologist-tested. Great choice for acne-prone skin.
Rhode Pocket Blush
Acne-Safe
Contains peptides and tamanu oil for hydration. Non-comedogenic, fragrance-free, paraben-free. Widely considered acne-safe by dermatologists.
About Tone Blur Powder Pact
Use With Caution
Talc-based pressed powder. While talc itself has a low comedogenic rating, layering pressed powder over oily skin throughout the day can create buildup. Contains dimethicone (comedogenic rating 1) and nylon-12.
⚠ Talc (can contribute to buildup)
⚠ Dimethicone (low risk, rating 1)
⚠ Coconut fruit extract (low concern in this form)
Too Faced Bronzer (Chocolate Soleil)
Use With Caution
Contains cocoa powder and fragrance which can be irritating to sensitive skin. Some formulations contain ethylhexyl palmitate (comedogenic rating 4). Check your specific variant's ingredient list.
⚠ Fragrance/cocoa scent (potential irritant)
⚠ Ethylhexyl palmitate in some variants (rating 4)
⚠ Talc base
Dae Hair Wand (for brows)
Acne-Safe
Hair styling product used on brows — minimal skin contact. Low risk as long as you're not pressing it into the skin heavily.
Benefit Benetint
Acne-Safe
Water-based tint with a very simple, lightweight formula. Considered acne-safe by multiple estheticians. Oil-free, silicone-free. Contains fragrance (parfum) which could irritate very sensitive skin, but the formula is minimal.
⚠ Fragrance/parfum (minor sensitivity risk)
⚠ Methylparaben (preservative, generally safe)
Summer Fridays Lip Butter Balm
Acne-Safe
Lips only — no comedogenic concern for facial skin. Contains shea butter and murumuru butter for hydration. Just avoid smearing it on your cheeks.
Kiss Me Heroine Make Mascara
Acne-Safe
Eye area only. Anhydrous (water-free) formula with conditioning oils like argan, camellia, and rosehip. Non-irritating formulation suitable for sensitive eyes.
Overall Assessment
Your makeup lineup is quite good for acne-prone skin. The Merit concealer and Rhode blush are both solid choices. The two products to keep an eye on are the About Tone powder (avoid heavy re-application — blotting papers are better for oil control) and the Too Faced bronzer (check your specific shade for ethylhexyl palmitate). Your lip, eye, and brow products are all low-risk since they don't sit on acne-prone areas.
